- **Helper text**: 0.75rem (12px), slate-400

## Spacing

- **Message padding**: 0.75rem (12px) vertical, 1rem (16px) horizontal
- **Message margin**: 1rem (16px) bottom
- **Component spacing**: 0.5rem (8px) between elements
- **Badge spacing**: 0.375rem (6px) gap

## Border Radius

- **Message bubbles**: 1rem (16px)

- **Badges**: 0.375rem (6px) or full (rounded-full)
- **Buttons**: 0.5rem (8px)
